About Us

A leading BPO Company since 1994, after acquiring rich experience in BPO and Transaction processing Services, ChoiceConnex offers Contact Center Solutions to Global companies on 24 X 7 basis. Our company is a corporate member of NASSCOM.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified Call Center.
  • ISO 27001:2013 certified Call Center.
  • Corporate Member of NASSCOM, CII & DMA (USA).
  • DoT & TRAI Licensed Domestic & International Call Center

ChoiceConnex is uniquely positioned to provide today's industry leaders with the advanced technology and outsourced services they need to communicate with their customers, anytime, anywhere, through any channel.

The best people caterign to your best queries

Our facility is equipped with important amenities to support hi- tech operations to support 24x7 operations .We have state-of-the-art infrastructure at one of the prime locations at CBD Belapur in Navi Mumbai. For a pleasing working atmosphere, there are comfortable individual workstations for our Customer Service Representatives (CSRs), spacious lounge with Customer Servicesealing ambience.

  • 01 Quality, KPI & Customer Satisfaction

    We have a structured Quality Management System in place with KPIs fixed for each of the major processes and the same being monitored regularly at every Management Review Meeting. Call monitoring and training are other areas where we lay utmost stress upon and are covered through a separate process in the Quality management System. We have a very sincere and dedicated team working for the maintenance and continual improvement of the Quality Management Team.

  • 02 Voice, Data and Telephony management systems

    Telephone support services, fully integrated with Internet support provides a consistent customer experience across all available contact channels, enabling closer management and measurement capabilities for our clients. ChoiceConnex provides a range of 24x7 in-bound and out-bound unified customer support and sales services for top corporations as well as new generation Internet/ technology companies.

  • 03 E-mail response management systems

    Timely email response is the starting point for CRM. ChoiceConnex provides a very reliable and flexible system that responds rapidly to customer queries. Our email response services range from simple courtesy messages to expert system-driven responses to customer inquiries. ChoiceConnex Agents are thoroughly trained and familiar with the client's products, and our knowledge base of frequently asked questions and answers are created with the client's input and are continually updated and improved based on our ChoiceConnex Agents interactions with customers.
